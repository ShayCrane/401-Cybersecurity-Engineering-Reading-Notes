## What is computer forensics?
***https://www.wgu.edu/blog/computer-forensics2004.html#close***
<br></br>

Computer forensic careers: 
*specialists investigate security issues, data breaches, and other cyber crimes.*
- Cybersecurity meets law enforcement, criminal justice, and forensics in this field
    - cyber crime
        - digital/electronic evidence recovery
            - documents, photos, emails, and other files from computer systems, hard drives, and other devices
    - data breaches
        - investigation in to breaches and the documentation to prevent them in the future

Some job titles associated with cyber forensics: 
- Information security crime investigator
- Computer forensics engineer

Duties: 
- Conduct data breach investigations
- Recover and examine data from computers or electronics
- Identify additional systems or networks that may have been compromised
- Compile evidence for legal cases
- Draft technical reports and write declarations to prepare evidence for trial.
